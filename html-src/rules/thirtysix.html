Thirty-Six

Klondike type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Rules

Cards are dealt to six tableau piles of six cards each. During the initial deal, any ace that would be dealt is instead moved to the foundation immediately. Tableau piles are built down by rank, regardless of suit.

Cards can be dealt from the talon one at a time. No redeal is allowed.

The foundations are built up by suit from ace to king. The game is won when all cards are moved to the foundations.
