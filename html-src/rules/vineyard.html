Vineyard

Baker's Dozen type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Baker's Dozen, but 10 piles, which are built down by alternate color, and any card can fill an empty space.

Notes

Vineyard was invented by Peter Voke, and was first created in the Solitaire Wizard of the Pretty Good Solitaire app. It has since been ported to many other solitaire apps.
