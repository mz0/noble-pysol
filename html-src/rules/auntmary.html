Aunt Mary

Klondike type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Klondike, but with a different layout, consisting of six piles of six cards each - all cards are face-up in the leftmost pile, and each pile to the right has one more face-down card at the bottom of it than the one to its left. Additionally, no redeals are allowed.
