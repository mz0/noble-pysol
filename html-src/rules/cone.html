Cone

Gypsy type. 2 decks. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Gypsy, but with seven tableau piles, with four cards in the middle one, and one fewer in each pile to the left and right (all cards dealt face-up). Also, cards cannot be dealt from the talon if there are any empty piles.

Additionally, when there are only four cards left in the talon, they can be dealt straight to four reserve piles, and freely moved to the tableau or foundations when possible.
