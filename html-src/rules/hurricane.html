Hurricane

Pairing game type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the single foundation.

Rules

Cards are dealt to ten piles of three cards each, along with three additional single cards. Pair off cards whose ranks total 14, and discard them to the foundation. Cards from the three single cards are replaced from the stock, but the piles of three are not replaced when emptied.

Aces have a value of one, jacks a value of of 11, queens are 12, and kings are 13.

The game is won if all cards have been discarded to the foundation.

Notes

Autodrop is disabled for this game.
