Firing Squad

One-Deck game type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Move all cards except the four Aces to the single foundation.

Quick Description

Like Aces Up, but with a single free cell added. Any card may be moved to this free cell, but a card in this free cell cannot be moved to the foundation or used to move another card to the foundation. It can only be returned to the tableau by being moved to an empty column.
