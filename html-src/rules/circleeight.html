Circle Eight

One-Deck game type. 1 deck. 1 redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the tableau.

Rules

Eight tableau cards are dealt in a circle around the talon. Cards are dealt from the talon one at a time and can be moved to tableau piles which are built up by rank, regardless of suit. Cards cannot be moved between piles. One redeal is allowed. The game is won if all cards are moved to the tableau circle.
