Hit or Miss Unlimited

One-Deck game type. 1 deck. Unlimited redeals.

Object

Move all cards to the foundation.

Quick Description

Just like Hit or Miss, but with unlimited redeals.

Notes

The traditional rules of Hit or Miss only allow you to go through the deck twice without getting a hit, but this rule is often overlooked due to the extreme difficulty of winning the game it causes. Most computer versions of Hit or Miss do not use this rule, but when this rule is omitted, the game is always won unless the player gets stuck with a multiple of 13 cards left. This version does not use this rule, while Hit or Miss does.
