Eight Cards (Acht Karten)

Pairing game type. 1 deck. No redeal.

Object

Discard all the cards.

Rules

Eight cards are dealt to the tableau. Cards with a total rank of 11 are removed, and replaced from the tableau. Picture cards can be removed on their own. Up to twice during the game, you are allowed to draw a single card from the talon as a ninth card, which can then be paired with an appropriate tableau card.

You win when the tableau piles are all gone.

Notes

Autodrop is disabled for this game.
