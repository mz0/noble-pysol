Lady Jane

Klondike type. 2 decks. 1 redeal.

Object

Move all cards to the foundations.

Quick Description

Like Big Harp, but all cards are visible, tableau piles are built down by rank, regardless of suit, though only sequences of cards of the same suit can be moved together (in a similar manner to Spider. Also, cards are dealt from the talon three at a time with only one redeal allowed.
