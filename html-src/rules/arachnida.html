Arachnida

Spider type. 2 decks. No redeal.

Object

Group all the cards in sets of 13 cards in descending sequence by suit from King to Ace and move such sets to the foundation.

Rules

Cards are dealt to ten tableau piles - four with six cards and six with five. Only the top card of each pile is face-up. Tableau piles are built down by rank or by same rank. Only single cards or sequences of cards that are the same rank or built down by suit can be moved between tableau piles.

When there are no moves left, you can deal one card from the talon to each tableau pile.

When a same suit sequence is created that goes all the way from king to ace, it is immediately moved to a foundation pile. The game is won if all cards are moved to this foundation pile.
